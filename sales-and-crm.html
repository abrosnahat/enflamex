Our Advantages

Our CRM is going to boost the performance of any sales team, and will help sales people to excel in 4 areas that are critical for them:

  • better search, sort and qualify leads;
  • follow up on sales opportunities systematically and on time;
  • prioritize and rationalize follow up activities;
  • and increase target reach rates faster;

Also, sales teams need to synchronize their activities and base them on best practices. This will enable a better sharing of information and will secure more effective work with CRM.

Customizable to fit your sales cycle

Easily tailor your CRM software to work for you, without any development help. Edit deal stages, add as many columns as you'd like, manage multiple pipelines at once, and more.

Save valuable time by automating sales processes

Close more deals by automating repetitive work. Automatically assign leads to reps, set reminders for upcoming activities, get notified when a lead opens an email, and more.

Centralize client communication

Communicate efficiently with your contacts by integrating your email. Automatically log sent emails, keep track of interactions, and use personalized email templates to save you time.

Sales CRM empowers you to streamline sales activities from pre- to post-sales, creating a comprehensive and frictionless sales cycle.
